Chasing our goals, we present to you "The Travelling Gypsies". PARINDEY swears to make your traveling experience indelible. We will provide you with the best nomadic hostels in the zone at a pocket-friendly price, street or local food that you've never tried before, and a vibe that you'll not get anywhere else.

It all began with the childhood friendship of our founders, dating back to their early years. Traveling seemed to be in their blood, starting from the outskirts of their hometown of Rourkela, Odisha, all the way to the extremes of the Himalayas. They never let a holiday pass without embarking on a trip, whether it was to a local waterfall or a small trek in the outskirts. These boys were so passionate about traveling that it eventually became their obsession. They explored various places, from dancing under waterfalls to sipping beer at sunset viewpoints. After all, they were teenagers. Then came a crucial moment when all of us were confined to our homes. What happens when you lock up eagles, or in this case, 'parindeys,' in a cage?

The next thing we knew, they had their first big break - their first official group trip to Puri and their first excursion beyond their hometown. In their own way, they achieved a significant milestone. And, boy, was it unforgettable. They were now fired up and ready to explore beyond their wildest dreams, from Manali in the north to Rajasthan in the west, Munnar in the south, and Gangtok in the east.They started their YouTube channel, which garnered them considerable appreciation. Their concept of budget-friendly, gypsy-like traveling was lauded and followed by many. They received dozens of requests from people to either join their trips or share their itineraries, which was quite unique. In the post-COVID era during their college years, they met fellow adventurers like themselves, and together they embarked on marvelous trips that defy description. These friends explored uncharted territories and engaged in activities that could be considered milestones, setting important benchmarks for both travel and friendship.With all of this in mind and the positive feedback they received, they decided to create a platform to connect travelers, cater to their needs, and build a community of like-minded explorers. All of these experiences culminated in the formation of TRAVELLING GYPSIES by PARINDEY 7.
